What Are Trading Indicators?

Trading indicators are mathematical tools that analyze historical price movement. They help traders observe market structure, trend behavior and momentum strength. Indicators should always be used as supporting tools, not as standalone trading decision systems.

  • Trend indicators help identify market direction
  • Momentum indicators measure buying and selling pressure
  • Structure levels show important price zones
  • Multiple indicator confirmation is recommended
