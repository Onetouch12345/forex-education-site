What Are Forex Indicators?

Forex indicators are mathematical tools used to analyze price movement behavior. They help traders identify trend direction, momentum strength and market structure patterns. Indicators should always be used as supporting decision tools rather than standalone signals.

  • Trend indicators show market direction
  • Momentum indicators measure buying and selling pressure
  • Multiple indicator confirmation is recommended
