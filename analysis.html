Global Forex Market Overview

The foreign exchange market is the largest financial market in the world with over $7 trillion traded daily. Professional traders analyze the market using three primary methods:

  • Technical analysis – chart patterns, indicators and price structure
  • Fundamental analysis – interest rates, inflation and economic data
  • Market sentiment – investor risk appetite and capital flow

Successful traders combine these approaches to develop a probability-based trading strategy.

Technical Analysis Basics

Technical analysis studies price behavior using charts and indicators. Professional traders typically analyze:

  • Support and resistance levels
  • Trend structure (higher highs / lower lows)
  • Moving averages
  • Momentum indicators such as RSI and MACD

Indicators should be used as confirmation tools rather than trading signals. Risk management is the most important factor in long-term trading success.

Risk Management Principles

  • Never risk more than 1-2% of account per trade
  • Always use stop losses
  • Avoid overtrading
  • Focus on consistency rather than quick profits

Professional trading success depends more on discipline and capital protection than prediction accuracy.
