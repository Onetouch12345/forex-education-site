What is Forex Trading?

Forex trading is the process of buying and selling currencies in the global foreign exchange market. It is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world.

How Forex Market Works

The Forex market operates 24 hours a day, five days a week. Currencies are traded in pairs such as EUR/USD or GBP/USD.
